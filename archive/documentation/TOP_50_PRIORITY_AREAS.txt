For each area name above, collect:

   - Right-click on area center
   - Copy coordinates (e.g., "25.0714, 55.1433")

2. Distance to Nearest Metro (km)
   - Use Google Maps "Measure distance"
   - Find closest Red Line or Green Line station

3. Distance to Nearest Beach (km)
   - Measure to JBR, Kite Beach, La Mer, etc.

4. Distance to Nearest Major Mall (km)
   - Dubai Mall, Mall of Emirates, Marina Mall, etc.

5. Distance to Nearest School (km)
   - Any major school (or use 10.0 as default)

6. Distance to Business District (km)
   - Business Bay, Downtown, DIFC, Marina, JLT, etc.

7. Neighborhood Score (1-5)
   - 5.0 = Exceptional (Palm Jumeirah, Emirates Hills)
   - 4.5 = Excellent (Marina, Downtown)
   - 4.0 = Very Good (JLT, Business Bay, Arabian Ranches)
   - 3.5 = Good (Most established areas)
   - 3.0 = Average (Standard residential)
   - 2.5 = Below Average (Developing areas)
