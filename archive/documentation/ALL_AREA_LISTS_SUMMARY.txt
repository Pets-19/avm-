================================================================================
DATA FORMAT REMINDER
================================================================================

You need 8 fields for each area:

1. Area Name (copy exactly from list)
2. Latitude (e.g., 25.0714)
3. Longitude (e.g., 55.1433)
4. Metro Distance (km) - to nearest metro station
5. Beach Distance (km) - to nearest beach
6. Mall Distance (km) - to nearest major mall
7. School Distance (km) - to nearest school (or 10.0 default)
8. Business Distance (km) - to business district
9. Neighborhood Score (1-5) - quality rating

**CSV Format:**
```csv
Area Name,Latitude,Longitude,Metro Distance (km),Beach Distance (km),Mall Distance (km),School Distance (km),Business Distance (km),Neighborhood Score (1-5)
Jumeirah Lakes Towers,25.0714,55.1433,0.5,8.5,3.2,2.0,4.5,4.2
```
